Bikes

Most of my free time is spent with bikes - if I'm not riding one or wrenching, I'm thinking about them. I volunteer once a week at the Bikerowave Co-Op as a Head Mechanic. This page includes a few of the bikes I ride these days. If its not abundantly evident, I'm a big retro bike nerd.

1997 Ibis Hakkalugi

My favorite bike, it can tackle pretty much anything I encounter on a regular LA ride. Triple butted steel, tubeless tires, and 28 spoke wheels converge into a very plush ride - plus its light (frame & fork < 4lbs!) and can fit 700 x 42s! My only complaint is the lack of mounting points on this particular model, but I plan on getting some brazed on in the future.


Frame: 1997 Ibis Hakkalugi
Fork: Independent Fabrication CX
Wheels: DT Swiss RR 511 & Chris King R45D
Tires: Schwalbe G-One 700 x 35
Cockpit: Thomson X2 & Zipp SL 70 XPLR
Brake Levers: Ultegra 6700 (previously Dura Ace 7800)
Brakes: Paul Mini-Moto front, Touring Canti rear
Headset: Chris King
Crankset: Middleburn Compact Double
Pedals: MKS Gamma
Front Mech: Ultegra 6700
Rear Mech: RD-M950 XTR
Seatpost: Dean Ti (previously Syncros ti)
Saddle: Selle Italia Flite
